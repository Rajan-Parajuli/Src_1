Rules

1. Public Records Act, 1993

The act provides for the management, preservation, and disposal of public records. It mandates government offices to maintain and manage records in an orderly manner.

2. Right to Information (RTI) Act, 2005

Citizens have the right to request government documents and information under this act. It promotes transparency and accountability.

3. Official Secrets Act, 1923

It restricts access to sensitive government information related to national security, defense, and foreign relations.

4. General Financial Rules (GFR)

Provides guidelines for managing financial documents and records in government offices.

5. Electronic Records Management

Includes guidelines for creating, storing, and using digital documents securely under the Information Technology Act, 2000.
